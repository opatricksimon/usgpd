Our Mission & Structure

Promoting the peace department concept globally

Leadership and Authority

The head of this department would be a secretary or cabinet level appointment whose sole role would be to provide to the President and government a peace path for all engagements locally, regionally and internationally.

Department Structure

Built out like a foreign service with a local bureau for domestic issues. This department would have a corresponding unit to engage along with its foreign service counterpart.

Funding Strategy

The department would receive direct annual funding from the national budget for all its activity domestic and abroad to ensure sustainable operations.

Expanded Sub Units

The Peace Department shall establish units within every department and agency, including entities of the government i.e schools, and use these units as satellite for intervention.

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

The U.S. Global Peace Department (USGPD) is a research and strategic consulting organization that provides high-end guidance to governments and organizations worldwide. We focus on peace building, strategic leadership, corporate governance, and conflict resolution through our "Big Picture" approach to expanded consciousness and sustainable development.

Our programs are designed for government officials, corporate executives, NGO leaders, educators, and anyone in a leadership position who wants to develop strategic thinking and peace-building skills. The "1000 Points of Light" program is free and open to grassroots leaders and community organizers.
